PantherX Operating System

for Panther Alpha

PantherX OS is a modern, {% include tooltip.html keyword="Unix-like" description="PantherX is a upstream distribution of GNU Guix and uses the Linux Kernel" %} computer operating system, designed to enable everyone without a PHD in computer science, to take control of their computer, data and privacy, and exercise their right to freedom from monitoring and tracking.

With a efficient {% include tooltip.html keyword="desktop environment" description="We proudly build on LXQt with PantherX-specific enhancements." %}, a set of highly integrated {% include tooltip.html keyword="applications" description="We have developed a number of apps specifically for PantherX, including Hub, Contacts, Calendar, Software - many of which you can easily extend yourself." %}, and access to thousands more trough Software, PantherX is set to become your next, and last (Desktop) OS.

  • A familiar environment with whatever you need for whatever you do
  • It's yours the minute you install it, and nobody will ever know about it
  • System updates never leave you with a broken system (1)
  • There's nothing to login to, or register for. No trials and nagging upgrade reminders
  • It's easy to replicate your whole, or parts of your system, to another computer
  • You can write and publish your own plugins for Accounts, Hub, Settings, ...
  • We're just getting started ...

(1) If they do (shi.. happens), experience your first, instant roll-back, and continue like nothing happened.

The Panther Vision

A brighter future for all.

  • Less waste: Make Desktop and Laptop up-gradable for non-techies.
  • Less drain: A Panther Alpha plus 21inch screen use less than 35W.
  • Less noise: A silent computer, and a desktop environment that stays out of the way.
  • More privacy: A OS that doesn't track you, and apps that don't phone home
  • More performance: Lightweight apps that focus on key features
  • More accessibility: Great software that doesn't cost anything, and that you can improve yourself.
  • More decentralization: Bitcoin was revolutionary, but other tech should follow the foot-steps.

It's our mission to provide you with a operating system and hardware, that satisfy both your today's and tomorrow's computing needs, and provide a steady stream of updates, so you're not only on the safe site, but receive new features regularly.
